## Questions to Ask Auditors

1. **Experience:**
   - How many Move/Aptos projects have you audited?
   - Can you share references or past reports?

2. **Process:**
   - What's your audit methodology?
   - Do you use automated tools + manual review?
   - What's your typical severity classification?

3. **Deliverables:**
   - What's included in the final report?
   - Do you provide a public summary?
   - Can we reference your audit in marketing?

4. **Timeline:**
   - When can you start?
   - What's the total duration?
   - How quickly can you do re-audit?

5. **Pricing:**
   - What's included in the base price?
   - What would trigger additional costs?
   - Do you offer ongoing retainer services?

6. **Support:**
   - Do you provide post-audit support?
   - Can you help with remediation?
   - Do you offer incident response services?

---

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ **Avoid firms that:**
- Have no Move/Aptos experience
- Provide quotes without seeing code
- Promise "guaranteed security"
- Offer suspiciously low prices (<$20K)
- Have no public track record
- Don't provide sample reports
- Refuse to sign NDA
- Can't provide references

---

## Post-Audit Actions

After receiving audit report:

1. **Triage Findings** (Day 1)
   - Critical: Fix immediately
   - High: Fix before launch
   - Medium: Fix or document mitigation
   - Low/Info: Consider for future

2. **Remediation** (Week 1-2)
   - Implement fixes
   - Add tests for vulnerabilities
   - Update documentation

3. **Re-Audit** (Week 3)
   - Submit fixes for review
   - Get sign-off on critical issues
   - Receive final report

4. **Public Disclosure** (Launch)
   - Publish audit report
   - Add badge to website/docs
   - Share on social media
